# Fashion_store_data_analysis_phase1
Fashion Sales Analysis and Forecasting – README
Project Overview
This project analyzes historical customer transactions for a fashion retailer and forecasts sales for the next six months across four product categories: Clothing, Accessories, Footwear, and Outerwear. The work combines Power BI dashboards for descriptive analytics with Python models for time‑series forecasting.
​
Objectives
Understand customer behavior by age, gender, location, and subscription status.​

Identify high‑revenue categories, sizes, colors, and items.​

Analyze seasonal trends and purchase patterns.
​
Predict category‑wise sales for the upcoming six months to support inventory and marketing planning.​

Data Sources
Customer Table (public customer) – demographics, purchase history, review ratings, subscription status, payment and shipping preferences.​

Forecast Table (forecast_all_categories) – monthly predicted sales by category (Future_Date, Predicted_Sales, Max Predicted Sales per category).​

Key Tools and Technologies
Python: Time‑series forecasting of category‑wise sales using past sales data.
​
Power BI: Interactive dashboards, KPI cards, and visualizations for current and forecasted performance.

Main Insights
Clothing and Accessories are the largest revenue drivers today and in the six‑month forecast window.​
Male customers generate higher spending across all categories despite a balanced gender split.​
Most sales occur without discounts, and higher review ratings are associated with slightly higher purchase amounts.​
Seasonal effects are relatively balanced, enabling stable year‑round planning.
